Swim to the island

You swim towards the smoke stack where you find an island. When you arrive, the crew including yourself are exceedingly tired. You adjust your eyes to a fire alit on the beach. Several people dressed with loincloths and armed with spears dance around the fire and call out in a tongue unknown. You signal your crew to stay quiet while they commit their ritual. Soon, who looks like the tribe leader walks to the center of the circle and holds an idol made of wood over the fire. The people around the circle raise their arms and chant until the leader drops the idol in the fire, and the tribe quiets down. You stand up and make you and your crew visible to the tribe. They see you and form a circle around you, pointing their spears towards you and your crew. Before they can enunciate anything, however, the leader calls out, and the tribe lowers their spears slowly. He walks slowly to you and bows before your feet. The remainder of the tribe bows to your crew. “What’s going on?” one of the crew shouts. Another says “I think they think we’re what they were prayin’ about”. They carry you and your crew to the village, where you and your crew are dressed, cleaned, and praised in homage to their god.
