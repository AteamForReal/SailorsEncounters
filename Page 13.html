Your new job! Hahahahaha...

Your crew signals toward the pirate ship, crying for mercy. The pirate ship abruptly stops, creaking the wood of the hull and splashing the water off the bow. One by one, you and your crew are carried aboard. They apprehend your crew and stuff you all into their shockingly small brig. You fall asleep in the cage and are just glad that you’re going somewhere. When you awaken, you and your crew are hauled off the ship bound with chains on your wrists and ankles. Soon, you stand on a podium with three other of your crew. Still dreary, you see someone in the crowd in front of you yell out something and point at you. When you awake for the second time, You are in royal chambers asleep in a master bed. The decorations are beautiful, the room is wide, and your body is surprisingly…clean. Soon after you awake, someone bursts into your room with colorful clothes in his hand. “Dress up, I expect you in front of the throne in thirty minutes”. He tosses the clothes towards you and they land on the floor with a jingle. You pick up a puffy hat, and hold it up. Parts of the hat angle in different directions and bells jingle off each end. “Oh no” you say. You look behind you and a plaque is nailed to the bed frame: “Jester of His Majesty George II”.
