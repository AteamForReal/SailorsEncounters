Beach camp

The fire made on the beach runs late into the night, and you sit on a log in the circle surrounding it. The cartographer approaches you with several pieces of parchment, and discusses with you possible routes as he points out constellations in the sky that could guide us home. Before long, you hear someone near the water cry out “A ship approaches!”. You stand up and walk towards the shore, and you hear the creaking of the wood and the laughing of the people aboard before you can see the size of the hull. You back towards the fire as its light exposes the black sails and the pirate flag waving in the wind. “Whadoo we do, Captain?” several men shout.
