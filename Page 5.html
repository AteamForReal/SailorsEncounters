Ship gets closer

Continuing your ship’s trajectory despite the unexpected encounter, all seems well for a short while. However, you notice that the ship has gotten closer since you spotted them. They seemed to be more in front than on the left, as if they were preparing to cut you off. You observe the ship at the bow while your crew observes with you. One of the crew next to you points out that they’re raising the sails, and upon closer inspection for yourself, you observe the same. Eventually, the man in the crow’s nest shouts for all below to hear: “The colors have risen! Pirate party ahead!”. The crew now stir up panic amongst themselves as you take out your telescope to see for yourself. Sure enough, a flag had been raised, equipped with the trademark of a skull and a silver hook caressing it. As you put away your telescope, you gain the anxious eyes of your crew as they switch between looking at the ship and looking at their captain. Your first mate shouts from the deck, “Orders, sir?”.

  • Raise the white flag and surrender.
  • Fight! Dust off and arm the cannons.
