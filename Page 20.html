Pirates offer you a drink

Upon declining the offer, the pirate captain goes back into his quarters and returns with two bottles filled to the neck. He pops the cork and requests that you sip. You slowly take the bottle and sip. You wince back but secretly delight in the drink. The pirate Captain chuckles then snatches the bottle from your grasp with his hook. The pirates offer you what they claim is the “finest ale on this side of the Atlantic”. You turn to your crew and they shrug their shoulders. You turn back and the Captain says “I understand the situation I’ve placed ya in. I’ll let ya think on it should ya accept a few barrels o’ this ‘ere” as he waves the bottle of ale in front of your face.

  • You accept the offer of ale, but decide to "sleep on it"
