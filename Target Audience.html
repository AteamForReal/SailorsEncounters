Target Audience

This game, titled "Sailor's Encounters", is intended for all ages. The content of this game includes pirates,
native tribes, and merchant sailor's seeking fortune on the high seas. The only significant mature theme
included involves the consumption of alcohol, but there is no explicit drunkeness. Should your child understand
the risks and factors involved in consuming alcohol, they will be more than suitable to play this game.
