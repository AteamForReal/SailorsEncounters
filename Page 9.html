Discover the tribe

You and the small huddle of crewmates walk into the brush, and follow a sandy path made by nature. Before long, a crewmate looks over to see something red sticking out of a tree. He goes over and plucks it out. “It’s a dart”, he says. You and your crew continue walking, but with increased awareness of your circumstance. Eventually you come upon an open area with several huts surrounding the land. One of your crew taps on your shoulder. He points to the opposite end of the village towards a small group of men approaching slowly. He asks you “What’s the plan, Cap’n? Are we to fraternize with these folk or not?”
