Decline offer from tribe, camp on the beach

You kindly decline the offer from the tribe leader under hidden suspicion. You and your crew return to the beach, discussing the manner of the tribe's demeanor. While you are talking with your crew, one among the group waves in front of your face with a finger and signals to quiet down. He points to the beach and you notice several people sleeping around a burnt-out campfire. You look at the shore and see a giant dark ship disguised in the night. You and your crew murmur among yourselves while you tiptoe across the beach. As you approach the ships, someone emerges from the water with a jug in his hand and sword drawn. He waves his weapon around without aim, mumbles a few words loudly, then falls into the sand. You and your crew gaze upon the ship. “Whaddya think we should do with this here, Cap’n?”.

  • Attempt to steal the pirate ship and escape
  • Sleep nearby on the border of the woods, where they cannot see you
