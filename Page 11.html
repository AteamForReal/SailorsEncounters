Accepted Alliance

You have accepted alliance and service to Captain Chrome-Hook, and he welcomes you aboard the ship. Other than a rough branding on the rear and initiation, your crew and yourself seem happy for the time being. You remain with your ship, but now you wave a flag allied with Chrome-Hook’s and a share of the fish you catch has to go to him. He also gives you maps towards the neighboring lands and grants good luck with your hunting. Before you take off, he demands to handle the white flag, and with his hook, he slashes it and throws it into the water. “No quitters for Chrome-Hook. Happy sails.”
