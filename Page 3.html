Ship Sighted!


Upon turning left, the crew shields their eyes from the light of the sun and you continue to stare out into the water. Not too much later does an exclamation sound from the crow’s nest. “Ship sighted, port side!”, he declares, and the crew rushes to the left side of the ship, and you nearly lose your balance from the shift in weight. You climb up the steps to the upper deck as your first mate squints his eyes, holding one hand over his brow and the other onto the ship’s wheel. You yourself squint to view the ship’s dark sails and elongated hull. You and your crew take a long look as the ship’s bow is angled in front of your trajectory. While your first mate still gazes, he asks, "What should we do, Captain?"

  • Maintain Course.
  • Retreat.
