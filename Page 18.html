Paradise with the Natives

You’ve accepted the tribe’s offer, and now you are staying in their huts. You are given bunks to share with your crew, and showers to clean yourself with. Later in the evening, one of the crew announces that the tribe is having a bonfire, and half of the crew rushes out to join. You decide to join, and the tribe accepts your crew with open jugs and ripe fruits. After a night of partying with the tribe, you and your crew return to the huts. When you awake, however, you find your ship and a pirate ship sailing away. You and your crew decide that paradise dwells here, and you stay with the tribe.
