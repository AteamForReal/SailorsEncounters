About the Author

Aaron T. Davidson grew up in Lakeland, Florida and goes to the University of Central Florida in Orlando.

He has two siblings and a dog. Aaron loves to play guitar, write stories, and collect baseball cards.

Aaron is currently studying for a Bachelor of Arts degree in Digital Media with a minor in Creative Writing.

In the future, he hopes to co-found a video game company with his roommate and seeks to write his stories
for not only video games, but also movies, books, and any other form of entertainment that he seeks to serve.
