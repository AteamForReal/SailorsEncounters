Praised in homage

You awake to find both ships gone with half of your crew. You turn to view the border of the forest to face a group of tribal members walking to you. They form a circle around you, pointing their spears towards you and your crew. Before they can enunciate anything, however, the leader calls out, and the tribe lowers their spears slowly. He walks slowly to you and bows before your feet. The remainder of the tribe bows to your crew. “What’s going on?” one of the crew shouts. Another says “I think they think we’re what they were prayin’ about”. They carry you and your crew to the village, where you and your crew are dressed, cleaned, and praised in homage to their god.
