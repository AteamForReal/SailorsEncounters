Offer of the Natives

The natives, upon your offer, accept and give you food and supplies for you and your crew. You give them an extended thank you and a series of bows to make sure you respect them. They return as welcome, and offer for you to stay in their homes. You turn to discuss with your crew. “I don’t know, Cap’n”, one of them says, “they could be out to sacrifice us or sumthin”. Another says “With how my back feels, I think it’s worth it to feel a bed again. I say "someone just keep watch and we’ll be alright”. They all look at you for a response.

  • Stay in tribe houses for the night
  • Return to the beach
