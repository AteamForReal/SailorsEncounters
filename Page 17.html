Offer of Recruitment on the beach

Two pirates hop off of the ship and walk towards you. The rest of your crew back up the beach while you stand firm. Soon, a ladder is cast over the ship and a coated man descends it. He approaches you. His beard is gray and long, barnacles were growing on the tail of his hat and coat, but his hook blinds everyone with its silver shine. “You the Cap’n?” he asks you, and you nod while you stand a foot from the chrome-hooked man’s seafoam eyes. “I take that as-a-yes”, he says. “Well, I think ya guys arr in quite a tight spot. I can get ya out of it in two ways, but I’ll onleh tell ya one. Accept your new life as a pirate, and you’ll make this a lot easier on yaselves”. The captain fiddles with his hook.
