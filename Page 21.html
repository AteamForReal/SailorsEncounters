Ship Stolen!

You and your crew successfully steal the ship and escape with one more than you had before! All crew is well and you sail off with proper guidance from the pirate’s maps. You and your crew decide to go fishing again with the enhanced equipment from the pirate’s ship. You catch bundles and hordes of trouts, cod, and salmon until your crew grows sore from the work. You return both ships to main land and sell the fish for a fortune. You split the riches with your crew and celebrate at the pub, then return to the pirate ship and sell “The Righteous Pike”. You decide to pursue your dream as a corn cutter (podiatrist).
